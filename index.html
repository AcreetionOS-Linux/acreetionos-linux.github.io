AcreetionOS is an approachable and user-friendly distribution based on the powerful Arch Linux core. We aim to make the Arch ecosystem accessible to a wider audience by providing a stable, pre-configured desktop experience.

This project is made possible by the incredible work of the Arch Linux developers. We simply add a layer of convenience and testing to an already fantastic base.

Our Philosophy & How You Can Help

We Are Open & Independent

Everything related to AcreetionOS, including this website, is fully open-source. We are a small, passionate team of three full-time and one part-time developer. Our independence is key—we are self-hosting our infrastructure and want to avoid corporate overlords dictating our direction.

To fund our project without compromising our values, we are exploring the creation of proprietary software for other operating systems. This allows our Linux-based work to remain free and open, forever.

How We Work

As a small team, we use a modern, centralized workflow that fits our needs. We use our Discord server for community interaction and as a replacement for GitHub issues, keeping everything in one place. We also utilize Nextcloud for internal collaboration and are setting up a Discourse instance for community forums.

How You Can Contribute

We are actively looking for contributors and sponsors to help us grow. Scaling up takes time, and support from the community is crucial. We currently only have one download mirror and are looking for sponsors to help us expand our infrastructure.

  • Developers: We welcome all contributors, including those who wish to contribute via AI-assisted tools. If you want to help, please join the Discord or email us at developers@acreetionos.org.
  • Sponsors: We are looking for sponsors to provide resources like mirrors, not to tell us what to do. If you can help, please get in touch.

Using Your Own Flashing Tool

After downloading an ISO, you will need to write it to a USB drive (8GB or larger recommended). This will erase all data on the USB drive.

Balena Etcher (Recommended for most users)

Balena Etcher is a simple graphical tool for Windows, macOS, and Linux. 1. Select the downloaded AcreetionOS ISO file. 2. Select your USB drive. 3. Click "Flash!".

Ventoy (for multi-boot USBs)

Ventoy is a tool that lets you create a bootable USB drive where you can simply copy multiple ISO files. 1. Install Ventoy to your USB drive once. 2. Drag and drop the AcreetionOS ISO file onto the USB. 3. Boot from the USB to see the Ventoy menu.

Using `dd` (Advanced Linux Users)

The `dd` command is a powerful but dangerous tool. Incorrect usage can lead to data loss. Be certain of your USB drive's device name (e.g., `/dev/sdb`, not `/dev/sdb1`).

sudo dd if=/path/to/AcreetionOS.iso of=/dev/sdX bs=4M status=progress oflag=sync

Replace `/path/to/AcreetionOS.iso` with the actual path to your downloaded file and `/dev/sdX` with your USB device identifier.

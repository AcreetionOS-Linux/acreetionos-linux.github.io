Bug Reports & Feature Requests

The only official way to submit bug reports and feature requests is by creating a ticket in our Discord server. We do not accept bug reports on GitHub.

Why We Use Discord for Support

You might wonder why we handle all support through Discord. As a project with code hosted across multiple platforms (like GitHub and private servers) and a community that will span several chat services (Discord, Matrix, XMPP), we've made a deliberate choice to centralize our core communications.

Using Discord as our central hub ensures that every piece of feedback—from bug reports to feature ideas—is seen, tracked, and discussed in one unified place. This prevents valuable community input from getting lost, keeps our small development team synchronized, and creates a single, vibrant community where users and developers can collaborate effectively.

By creating a ticket on Discord, you're directly entering our primary workspace, guaranteeing the fastest and most organized response. We appreciate you helping us keep our workflow streamlined!
